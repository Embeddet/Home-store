About ElectroStore

Your trusted partner in electronics innovation

Our Story

Founded in 2015, ElectroStore has been at the forefront of providing high-quality electronics components to makers, engineers, and hobbyists worldwide. What started as a small passion project has grown into a trusted supplier of premium electronic components.

We specialize in lithium batteries, Arduino boards, sensors, and various embedded system components. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred choice for both individual makers and large-scale manufacturers.

Our Mission

To empower innovation by providing reliable, high-quality electronic components and exceptional customer service to creators and engineers worldwide.
